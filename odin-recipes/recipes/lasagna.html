Ingredients

Here's what you'll need to create a mouthwatering lasagna:

For the Meat Sauce:

  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 lb (450g) ground beef or a mix of beef and pork
  • 1 large onion, chopped
  • 2-3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 (28 oz / 800g) can crushed tomatoes
  • 1 (15 oz / 425g) can tomato sauce
  • 2 tbsp tomato paste
  • 1/2 cup (120ml) beef broth or red wine (optional)
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 1/2 tsp dried basil
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • A pinch of sugar (optional, to balance acidity)
